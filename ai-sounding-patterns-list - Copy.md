# AI-Sounding Patterns Detection List

## **Corporate/Marketing Jargon to Watch For:**

### **High-Impact Words:**
- leverage (most common - sounds like business jargon)
- utilize (very corporate)
- implement (corporate speak)
- facilitate (business jargon)
- optimize (overused marketing term)
- enhance (corporate buzzword)
- streamline (business jargon)
- synergize (corporate speak)
- empower (corporate buzzword)
- revolutionize/transform/innovate/disrupt (overused buzzwords)

### **Strategy/Process Words:**
- methodology (academic/corporate)
- framework (business jargon)
- approach (corporate)
- tactic (business jargon)
- strategic (overused)
- operational (corporate)
- systematic (academic)
- analytical (business jargon)

### **Technical/Business Words:**
- robust (corporate adjective)
- scalable (business buzzword)
- actionable (corporate)
- ecosystem (business jargon)
- holistic (corporate buzzword)
- comprehensive (overused)
- quantitative/qualitative (academic)
- empirical/theoretical/conceptual/pragmatic (academic)
- functional/procedural/technical/technological (corporate)
- digital/virtual/artificial/machine/automated/intelligent/smart (tech jargon)
- advanced/cutting-edge/state-of-the-art/next-generation/future-proof (overused)
- game-changing/breakthrough/pioneering/groundbreaking/innovative (overused)

### **Human-Friendly Alternatives:**

**Instead of "leverage" →** use, apply, maximize, create, build, develop
**Instead of "utilize" →** use, work with, make use of
**Instead of "implement" →** create, build, establish, set up
**Instead of "optimize" →** improve, enhance, make better, refine
**Instead of "streamline" →** simplify, improve, make easier
**Instead of "enhance" →** improve, strengthen, add to
**Instead of "facilitate" →** enable, allow, help, make possible
**Instead of "synergize" →** combine, work together, integrate
**Instead of "empower" →** enable, support, help, give tools
**Instead of "revolutionize" →** change, transform, improve significantly
**Instead of "strategic" →** planned, thoughtful, deliberate
**Instead of "tactical" →** practical, specific, targeted
**Instead of "methodology" →** approach, way of working, process
**Instead of "framework" →** structure, approach, way of organizing
**Instead of "robust" →** strong, reliable, solid
**Instead of "scalable" →** flexible, adaptable, growing
**Instead of "actionable" →** practical, useful, specific
**Instead of "ecosystem" →** community, network, environment
**Instead of "holistic" →** complete, whole, integrated
**Instead of "comprehensive" →** thorough, complete, detailed
**Instead of "quantitative/qualitative" →** measurable, observable, descriptive
**Instead of "empirical/theoretical" →** proven, tested, practical
**Instead of "conceptual/pragmatic" →** practical, realistic, sensible
**Instead of "functional/technical" →** working, effective, practical
**Instead of "digital/virtual" →** online, connected, automated
**Instead of "artificial/machine" →** automated, smart, efficient

## **Simple Detection Rules:**

1. **If it sounds like something from a business school textbook → Flag it**
2. **If it's a common corporate buzzword → Suggest human alternative**
3. **If it makes content sound like a marketing brochure → Make it conversational**
4. **If it uses passive voice excessively → Make it active and direct**
5. **If it has multiple buzzwords in one sentence → Break it up**
6. **If it sounds like AI wrote it → Humanize it**

## **Review Process:**

For each detected pattern:
1. **Identify the specific word/phrase**
2. **Find the context** (what's being discussed?)
3. **Suggest human alternative** (what would sound more natural?)
4. **Explain why the change works better**
